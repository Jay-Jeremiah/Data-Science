    Data Science is all about gathering data, analysis of the data and decision making
    It helps in decision making,predictive analysis and pattern discoveries

How does a data scientist work?
It requires one to be conversant with the following:
    Machine learning
    Statistics
    Programming
    Mathematics
    Databases

A data scientist should find patterns within the data, he organises data in an organised 
format.
